How can I validate my business idea before investing too much time and money?

Validating Your Business Idea:

  1. Market Research: Identify your target audience and analyze competitors
  2. Problem Validation: Conduct interviews to confirm the problem exists
  3. MVP Testing: Create a minimum viable product to test demand
  4. Pre-sales: Try selling before building the full product
  5. Landing Page: Gauge interest with a signup page
